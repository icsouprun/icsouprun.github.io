Souprun EVERY SUNDAY

*Except Christmas Sunday*

  • Food Collection

    6/7pm @ different food stores

    Foods (eg. sandwiches, salads, soups, breads, pastries, yoghurts, fruits) are collected from food stores close to the store's closing time and transported to the Imperial College Union kitchen (ICU Kitchen). This not only redistributes unwanted food, but also reduces food waste.

    Map of key locations & collection points







  • Drink Making

    6/7pm @ ICU Kitchen

    Hot drinks, which are tea, coffee and hot chocolate, are made in the Union Kitchen*.

    Drink Recipe:
    Hot water can be boiled using the kettle and pots. Below describes the other ingredients that are used for the different drinks. We make sure that everything is mixed well by borrowing the giant kitchen spoon.

    Coffee Chocolate Tea
    1/2 jar coffee powder
    1 cup sugar
    1/2 milk carton     		1 Cadbury choc. powder
    2 Sainsbury choc. powder
    1 milk carton     		1 silver pack tea
    1 cup sugar
    1/2 milk carton

    * The Union kitchen can be accessed through asking for the kitchen key from FiveSixEight. The ingredients and everything needed are taken from the storage area, which is accessed through a member in the key list.

  • Packing

    7:20pm @ ICU Kitchen

    All the food are usually organised into the following categories: ham and pork, vegetarian, chicken, fish, salads and soups, bread, and desserts.The food and drinks are then loaded into the van ready to leave. Before leaving the kitchen and the storage area are locked up.*

    * The driver will pick up the van and park it in the back entrance (first floor) of ICU, which leads directly outside Albert Hall. The keys for the storage and the kitchen are returned.

  • The Run

    7:50pm - 9:45pm
    @ ICU Kitchen / Lincoln's Inn Fields (LIF)

    At 7:50pm, we start the journey to the distribution point (LIF). To prevent disappointment of people missing food because we came early or of us coming late, we aim to arrive at exactly 8:30pm. Roles for who are in charge of distributing what are assigned in the bus ride. Afterwards, we usually mingle with the guests. :) Then we make the trip back.

  • Washing up

    ~10pm @ ICU Kitchen

    After returning back to the Union Kitchen, we wash up and return all the things into the storage area. Finally, we're done (:

    * The driver returns the van. The Union kitchen key is borrowed from FIveSixEight for access to the kitchen. A member in the key list will borrow and return the storage key.

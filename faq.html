FAQ

Have questions?

What happens if you don't collect enough food?
We would make our own sandwiches by buying ham, cheese and sometimes bread on the way back from the collections.

Map of nearby grocery stores & their opening times

Red markers show the food stores



What happens if you don't have enough supplies?
We usually buy in bulk and ensure that there are enough supplies. However, if we are really lacking, we would buy from the nearby grocery stores. Refer to the map above for where they are.

What do you do if something goes wrong during the van journey?
There is usually a document in the front compartment of the van with all the details of what you should do if something goes wrong.

What do you do if there is food leftover?
If there is food leftover, we usually donate the food to St. Mungo's - Edith Road Resettlement, which is on 147 Kings Street, W6 9XY.
